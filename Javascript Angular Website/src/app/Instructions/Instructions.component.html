Welcome to the movie predictor!
You will be able to go through movies and choose whether you would like to be recommended movies like this or not.
We have also added keyboard support so you can use the 1,2,3,4 keys to quickly choose.
If you have not seen a film before, you can just skip it.

After you have completed a few choices, enter a username and click complete to train your personal movie guesser
Now click "Build New AI" to get started!

If you have already trained an "AI", click on "View AI Results" and type in the username to see the bot's recommendations.
